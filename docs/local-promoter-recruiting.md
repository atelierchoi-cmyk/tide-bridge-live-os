# Local Promoter Recruiting Playbook

## Positioning line

Tide Bridge Live OS is a private B2B platform connecting K-POP live IP with verified global promoters and market demand data.

## Target promoters

Prioritize promoters with:

1. K-POP, Asian pop, anime, esports, or fan-meeting history.
2. Access to 1,000–7,000 capacity venues.
3. Clean payment and settlement history.
4. Local marketing and ticketing partnerships.
5. Ability to propose guarantee or co-promotion structures.

## First outreach email

Subject: K-POP live IP partnership for your market

Hi [Name],

I’m reaching out from Tide Bridge, a Korean live IP business platform connecting selected K-POP and Korean entertainment projects with verified local promoters.

We are currently building our private promoter network for concerts, fan meetings, showcases, and brand-linked live events across key markets.

If your team is open to reviewing Korean live IP opportunities for [Country/City], we would like to invite you to apply for partner access and share your recent live event track record.

Partner access allows selected promoters to:

- review curated Korean live IP packages,
- submit structured city/venue/guarantee offers,
- discuss market-specific routing,
- and move qualified projects into a private deal room.

Application link: [Insert URL]

Best,
Tide Bridge
